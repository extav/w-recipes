Bean Mix

Description

I made this recipe myself. It is very easy to make, and requires minimal time, effort, and cleanup while still making a ton of food. You may want to have a large pot. I don't have a picture of it right now, so please take this dog instead.

Ingredients

  • 2 can of beans
  • 1 can fire-roasted tomatoes
  • 1 can sliced potatoes
  • 1 cup frozen corn
  • spices
    • 3 tsp salt
    • 1 tsp oregano
    • 1 tsp literally anything

Instructions

  1. Dump it all in a big pot
  2. Cook on lowest heat you can manage for 30+ minutes (could go like 2 hours if u want, but stir it every now and again)
