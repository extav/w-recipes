Chicken Tinga (Internet Shaquille)

Description

Based on a video from Internet Shaquille, this makes a whole bunch of tasty protein filling. I replace the chicken with jackfruit.

Ingredients

  • 1 chopped onion
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2-3lbs jackfruit (replacing chicken thigh)
  • 1 can fire-roasted tomatoes
  • 2-5 canned chipotle peppers
  • spices
    • 3 tsp salt
    • 1 tsp cumin
    • 1 tsp oregano (search for mexican variant)
    • (optional) 1-2 bay leaves
    • (optional) 2 tsp chili powder
    • (optional) 1 tsp smoked paprika

Instructions

  1. Toss it all in the slow cooker and cook on high for 3 hours or low for 6.
  2. Shred it up
  3. Optional but recommended! Immersion blend the remaining sauce, then return the shreds to the crockpot and cook for 15m on high.
